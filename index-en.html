About

Benjamin Le Bars is a composer and sound designer, dedicated to video game creators and independent narrative projects. A multi-instrumentalist passionate about building immersive worlds through sound, he collaborates with independent teams to shape a strong sonic identity, in harmony with budget, timing, and storytelling constraints. His musical world draws inspiration from rock, jazz, modern orchestral music, and game soundtracks, creating rich, dynamic textures tailored to each atmosphere. Whether it’s an atmospheric soundscape, interactive effects, or a full soundtrack, every project is approached with both creative vision and technical precision, bringing universes to life and revealing their essence.
